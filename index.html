Frequently Asked Questions

🎟 Can I buy tickets at the gate?

Only if not sold out. We recommend buying online in advance.

🚗 Is there parking at the venue?

Yes, but limited. We encourage carpooling or public transport.

⛔ Can I leave and re-enter the festival?

Yes, as long as you wear your wristband at all times.

💸 Are refunds available?

Tickets are non-refundable unless the event is cancelled.
